How do I use the Enquiries form?

The Enquiries Page is a tool that users can use to ask miscellaneous questions about how clubs work at Westlake or how to use the website. To use the Enquiries Page form, all you must do is submit an answer including your question, as well as your school email in the relevant input box.

How do I leave my club?

To leave a club, you may either submit an enquiry through the above form, or email the teacher in charge of the relevant club. To find the teacher's email, first find the desired club in the Timetable webpage and it will be listed under its description.
