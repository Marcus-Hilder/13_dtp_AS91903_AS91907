Westlake Clubs are at the heart of student life, offering a wide range of opportunities to get involved, meet new people, and develop new skills. Whether you're interested in sports, arts, academics, or social causes, there’s a club for everyone. Our clubs are student-led and supported by staff, creating a space where ideas can grow and leadership can thrive. Joining a club is a great way to explore your passions, build confidence, and make the most of your time at Westlake. Get involved, stay connected, and be part of something bigger.
